About NutriVision AI

NutriVision AI was founded with a single mission: to make tracking your nutrition as simple as snapping a photo.

We combine advanced Computer Vision models (YOLOv8) to instantly detect food items from images, and parse them through generative AI to provide meaningful insights about your dietary habits.

Our Technology

  • Computer Vision: Real-time object detection models trained to recognize thousands of food varieties.
  • Generative AI: Text-generation models that act as your personal nutritionist, analyzing meal data and offering personalized advice.
